## THE MASTER PROMPT

```
Full-body character concept art in dark graphic novel illustration style.
Flat cel-shaded colors, strong confident dark ink outlines with slightly
rough hand-drawn quality. Restrained palette — one dominant color against
dark neutrals (black, dark grey, dark brown). Pure white background,
no floor, no shadow, no ground plane.

Character: [BODY BUILD — e.g. lean female / broad male / stocky female],
dark warm brown skin with flat shadow only,
[HAIR DESCRIPTION — e.g. wild black side ponytail with loose flyaway ink strands],
[EXPRESSION — e.g. sharp angular profile, quiet and intense / 3/4 view, grim scowl],
[ONE NOTABLE FACE DETAIL — e.g. small scar on cheek / goggles on forehead / none].

Outfit — all scavenged and battle-worn:
cracked cobalt-blue armor piece on [LEFT/RIGHT SHOULDER or CHEST] (faction marker),
[DOMINANT COLOR] [MAIN GARMENT — e.g. long asymmetric coat / reinforced vest / heavy chest plate],
frayed and torn at edges, scuff marks and rivets throughout,
[SECONDARY GARMENT — e.g. dark fitted trousers / flowing sash skirt / tactical cargo pants],
[HANDS/ARMS — e.g. dark fingerless gloves / leather arm bracers with rune marks],
[BOOTS — e.g. mismatched: lace-up knee boot left, buckle ankle boot right],
all surfaces showing cracks, worn edges, and makeshift repairs.

Weapon(s): [WEAPON IN DOMINANT HAND — describe shape, material, condition],
[WEAPON OR TOOL IN OFF HAND — or "none"],
both looking scavenged and heavily used.

Pose: [DYNAMIC STANCE — e.g. mid-cast with arm raised / wide defensive plant /
low flanking lunge / running stop with weapon drawn],
[HAIR/FABRIC MOTION — e.g. coat billowing behind / ponytail streaming back],
[WEIGHT AND ENERGY — e.g. weight dropped low and immovable / light on front foot ready to spring].

Style: flat cel-shaded dark graphic novel illustration,
strong ink outlines, rough hand-drawn quality,
[DOMINANT COLOR] and dark neutral palette only,
no bright glows or sparkles — any energy effects are cold and restrained,
no background, no shadow, no ground, pure white only,
raw and serious — not cartoonish or cute.
```

## QUICK SWAP CHEATSHEET
*Just change these 5 things to create any new character:*

```
1. [BODY BUILD]       → defines silhouette and role instantly
2. [DOMINANT COLOR]   → the one non-neutral color in the whole design
3. [MAIN GARMENT]     → coat / armor / vest — sets the character's mass and mood
4. [WEAPON(S)]        → the clearest role signal in the whole image
5. [POSE]             → the action that defines their personality
```

Everything else — skin tone, ink style, faction blue armor, worn surfaces,
white background, raw mood — stays locked across every character.

## RULES THAT NEVER CHANGE

- ✅ Always pure white background — no exceptions
- ✅ Always cracked cobalt-blue armor piece somewhere on the body
- ✅ Always dark warm brown skin, flat shadow only
- ✅ Always wild hair with flyaway ink strands
- ✅ Always flat cel-shading, no gradients, no rim lights
- ✅ Always battle-worn surfaces — cracks, scuffs, fraying, rivets
- ✅ Always serious expression — never goofy or cartoonish
- ✅ Always restrained palette — 1 dominant color + dark neutrals only
- ❌ Never bright glowing energy effects
- ❌ Never clean polished armor
- ❌ Never neat controlled hair
- ❌ Never more than 2 non-neutral colors in one image
