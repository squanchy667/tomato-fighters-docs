# TALAMH — Ranger · Tank · Magician
## Redesigned in "Blue" Graphic Novel Style
### Same crew. Same world. Same raw ink aesthetic.

---

## BLUE'S STYLE DNA — LOCKED FOR ALL 3 CHARACTERS

Every prompt below is engineered to match these exact qualities from Blue's design:

| Element | Blue's Spec |
|---------|-------------|
| **Line art** | Strong confident dark ink outlines, slightly rough/hand-drawn quality |
| **Coloring** | Flat cel-shading — minimal gradient, large clean color zones |
| **Color count** | Restrained palette — 1 dominant color + dark neutrals (black, dark grey, dark brown) |
| **Skin** | Dark warm brown tone, rendered with flat shadow only, no complex shading |
| **Armor** | Cracked, riveted, battle-worn — scuff marks and stress fractures visible |
| **Fabric** | Dark flowing elements (sash, wrap, cloak) with torn/frayed edges |
| **Hair** | Wild, windswept, loose flyaway ink strands — never neat or controlled |
| **Expression** | Profile or 3/4 view, minimal — intense, quiet, not cartoonishly exaggerated |
| **Pose** | Full body, weight in motion, dynamic but grounded — not floating |
| **Background** | Pure white (#FFFFFF) — no floor, no ground plane, no shadow, no environment |
| **Mood** | Raw, serious, gritty — not vibrant or cute |
| **Faction marker** | Each character has a piece of cracked cobalt-blue armor — same crew |

## 01 — RANGER
**Role:** Ranged DPS & Mobility
**Dominant Color:** Warm Yellow-Ochre
**Weapon:** Crossbow + Grappling Hook

---

```
Full-body character concept art in dark graphic novel illustration style,
matching the exact aesthetic of this reference: flat cel-shaded colors,
strong confident dark ink outlines with slightly rough hand-drawn quality,
restrained palette with one dominant color against dark neutrals,
pure white background with no floor shadow or ground plane.

Character: young lithe female warrior, agile lean build,
dark warm brown skin rendered with flat shadow only,
wild high side ponytail of bright warm yellow-ochre hair
with loose flyaway ink strands escaping in all directions,
sharp angular features in focused 3/4 profile view,
narrow intense eyes tracking a target, lips pressed together in concentration.

Outfit — all battle-worn and scavenged:
cracked cobalt-blue shoulder pauldron on left shoulder (faction marker),
sleeveless yellow-ochre leather vest with fraying edges and strap rivets,
dark fingerless gloves with exposed knuckles,
slim dark trousers with thigh wrap bandages and a small belt pouch,
mismatched boots — one lace-up dark leather knee boot,
one shorter ankle boot with buckle straps,
all surfaces showing scuff marks, worn edges, and makeshift repairs.

Weapon: compact scavenged crossbow in dominant hand,
bolt loaded and aimed forward at slight angle,
other hand gripping a coiled grappling hook launcher at hip,
ready to fire or swing.

Pose: dynamic mid-action ranged stance,
body turned sideways to minimize target profile,
dominant foot forward in a controlled lunge,
crossbow arm fully extended and aimed,
grapple hand pulled back at hip ready to throw,
wild hair streaming back as if mid-sprint stop.

Style: flat cel-shaded dark graphic novel illustration,
strong ink outlines, rough hand-drawn quality,
restrained warm yellow-ochre and dark neutral palette,
no background, no shadow, no ground, pure white only,
raw and serious — not cartoonish or cute.
```

## 02 — TANK
**Role:** Frontline Defender & Anchor
**Dominant Color:** Deep Rust-Red
**Weapon:** Massive War Hammer + Tower Shield

---

```
Full-body character concept art in dark graphic novel illustration style,
matching the exact aesthetic of this reference: flat cel-shaded colors,
strong confident dark ink outlines with slightly rough hand-drawn quality,
restrained palette with one dominant color against dark neutrals,
pure white background with no floor shadow or ground plane.

Character: large powerfully built male warrior, exaggerated broad muscular frame,
dark warm brown skin rendered with flat shadow only,
short wild dark hair cropped close on sides with a rough uneven top,
thick dark brow furrowed hard, wide jaw set in a grim determined scowl,
small battle scar crossing the left cheek, 3/4 front-facing view,
eyes forward and locked — this man does not move.

Outfit — all massively battle-worn and scavenged:
cracked cobalt-blue chest plate (faction marker) — same blue armor as Blue
but much larger, dented deeply with hammer impacts and slash marks,
heavy rust-red armored shoulder guards layered and riveted,
rust-red reinforced forearm bracers over dark wrapped hands,
dark tactical trousers with thick leather knee wraps and utility straps,
enormous heavy dark combat boots with reinforced toe caps,
all surfaces showing deep gouges, chain marks, and welded repairs.

Weapons: enormous scavenged war hammer gripped in right hand —
head is asymmetric salvaged metal with riveted spikes, wrapped chain hanging from it,
handle is thick dark wood bound tightly in leather strips;
large rectangular tower shield strapped to left forearm —
same cracked blue metal as the chest piece,
surface covered in dents, slash marks, and a rough rebel symbol scratched in.

Pose: immovable defensive anchor stance,
legs planted wide apart, body weight dropped low,
left arm thrust forward with shield raised and angled outward — blocking everything,
right arm pulled back with hammer raised high overhead — ready to bring it down,
head lowered slightly, glaring from under the brow directly at viewer,
hair wild and unbothered.

Style: flat cel-shaded dark graphic novel illustration,
strong ink outlines, rough hand-drawn quality,
restrained rust-red and dark neutral palette,
no background, no shadow, no ground, pure white only,
raw, heavy, serious — not cartoonish or cute.
```

## 03 — MAGICIAN
**Role:** Energy Caster & Utility
**Dominant Color:** Deep Purple
**Weapon:** Runic Gauntlet + Tether Device

---

```
Full-body character concept art in dark graphic novel illustration style,
matching the exact aesthetic of this reference: flat cel-shaded colors,
strong confident dark ink outlines with slightly rough hand-drawn quality,
restrained palette with one dominant color against dark neutrals,
pure white background with no floor shadow or ground plane.

Character: slender tall female warrior-mage, lean ethereal build,
dark warm brown skin rendered with flat shadow only,
long dark hair loose and wild — same flyaway ink strand quality as the reference,
some hair pulled back loosely, most flowing free in all directions,
small cracked round goggles pushed up on her forehead (not over eyes),
sharp angular features in focused side profile,
one eye barely visible, expression — quiet and calculating.

Outfit — all scavenged and rune-marked:
cracked cobalt-blue chest plate (faction marker) —
same blue armor as Blue but lighter, more angular, fitted to her lean frame,
deep purple long asymmetric coat over it — one side longer than the other,
frayed and torn at hem edges, no clasp, hanging open,
dark fitted trousers tucked into mismatched dark boots,
one boot lace-up to the knee, one ankle-height with a buckle,
purple-tinted leather arm bracer on dominant forearm
with faint rune scratch-marks etched into the surface,
dark fingerless gloves with knuckle wraps.

Weapons: dominant hand raised with a scavenged runic gauntlet —
dark metal over-glove with cracked purple energy visible through stress fractures,
energy not glowing brightly — just a cold restrained deep purple seeping through,
other hand gripping a compact tether device at waist level —
a cylindrical scavenged tool with wrapped grip and a hooked end,
used to pull enemies or anchor.

Pose: mid-cast controlled stance,
body weight shifted to back foot, front foot stepping forward,
dominant arm raised forward with gauntlet channeling energy toward target,
tether hand pulled back ready to deploy,
long coat billowing behind her from the force of the cast,
wild hair streaming back,
expression completely cold and focused.

Style: flat cel-shaded dark graphic novel illustration,
strong ink outlines, rough hand-drawn quality,
restrained deep purple and dark neutral palette,
energy effects are cold and minimal — no bright glows, no sparkles,
just dark purple seeping through cracks,
no background, no shadow, no ground, pure white only,
raw, precise, serious — not cartoonish or cute.
```
